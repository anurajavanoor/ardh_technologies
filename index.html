Our Services

⬡

Analog & Mixed-Signal Layout

Precision layout for analog, RF, and mixed-signal circuits with optimal matching, symmetry, and noise isolation across nodes from 180nm to 7nm.

✓

Physical Verification

Complete DRC, LVS, ERC, MRC, Antenna, and IR Drop sign-off verification to ensure clean, tape-out-ready designs.

◈

Tapeout Support

End-to-end tapeout support including GDS-in, mask creation, DSDP execution, and foundry submission coordination.

PDK QA & Validation
